What We Do

We supply and install custom frameless glass systems for both residential and commercial properties, utilizing high-quality materials and the latest construction methods.

For superior quality and finish, we manufacture all of our glass products from A-grade toughened safety glass. Frosted, tinted and other design options are available to compliment your decor and design requirements.

John Spencer - Owner and Operator

Our focus on communication, quality and service ensures you, our valued client, never have to worry about any part of your project – we are with you from start to finish, ensuring your complete satisfaction. We work primarily alongside designers, architects and builders to ensure your project runs smoothly and is compliant with current regulations and the building code. If you are managing your own project or just looking for ideas, we are more than happy to assist.

Principals we endorse and follow for the design of our frameless glass family of products are:

-Health and safety
-Spatial design and multi functionality
-Lifecycle cost
-Quality assurance
-Resource responsibility
